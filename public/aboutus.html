About

ThinkUp’25 is more than just an ideathon — it’s a launchpad for bold ideas, creative minds, and problem-solvers of tomorrow. Designed to inspire innovation across disciplines, ThinkUp brings together students, creators, and visionaries to shape solutions that matter.

This year, ThinkUp’25 offers two dynamic categories:

Technical Track – For the coders, engineers, and tech-enthusiasts ready to turn concepts into working prototypes, apps, or tech-driven solutions.

Innovation & Business Track – For the strategists, designers, and dreamers who think beyond code — focusing on business models, creative ideas, design thinking, and impactful solutions.

No matter your background, ThinkUp’25 gives you the stage to ideate, collaborate, and pitch your vision. With mentors, networking opportunities, and a chance to showcase your ideas, it’s not just a competition — it’s where the future begins.
