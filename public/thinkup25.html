About ThinkUp'25

ThinkUp'25 is the flagship ideathon of Mandsaur University's techno-cultural fest, where innovation meets execution. This premier competition challenges participants to think beyond conventional boundaries and develop groundbreaking solutions that can reshape industries and solve real-world problems.

Whether you're passionate about business innovation or cutting-edge technology, ThinkUp'25 provides the perfect platform to showcase your ideas, collaborate with like-minded innovators, and compete for substantial prizes while gaining invaluable experience in the startup ecosystem.

Join us in this journey of innovation where ideas transform into reality.

Submission Categories

Business Oriented Ideas Focus on market-driven solutions, business models, entrepreneurial ventures, and commercial innovations. Present ideas that address market gaps, improve business processes, or create new revenue streams.
Technical Ideas Showcase cutting-edge technology solutions, software innovations, hardware prototypes, and technical breakthroughs. Present ideas involving AI, blockchain, IoT, cybersecurity, or any emerging technology.

Competition Timeline

01
Registration Phase Registration opens on 20th September 2025 and closes on 30th September 2025. Submit your innovative idea with a detailed description including problem statement, proposed solution, target audience, and potential impact.
↳ Shortlisted teams will be announced on 30th September 2025
02
PPT Submission Phase From 1st October to 6th October 2025, shortlisted teams must submit their PPT with details on solution architecture, business model, implementation strategy, and outcomes.
↳ Finalist teams will be announced on 7th October 2025
03
Final Presentation Round On 15th October 2025, the top teams will present their ideas live. • On-Campus (Offline) – For Mandsaur University students. • Online – For students from other universities across India. This ensures nationwide participation.
↳ Compete for the grand prize before expert judges
