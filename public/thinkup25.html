About ThinkUp'25

ThinkUp'25 is the flagship ideathon of Mandsaur University's techno-cultural fest, where innovation meets execution. This premier individual competition challenges participants to think beyond conventional boundaries and develop groundbreaking solutions that can reshape industries and solve real-world problems.

Open to all university students across India from all courses, ThinkUp'25 provides the perfect platform to showcase your ideas, compete with brilliant minds nationwide, and win substantial prizes while gaining invaluable experience in the innovation ecosystem.

Join us in this journey of innovation where individual brilliance transforms ideas into reality.

Submission Categories

Business Oriented Ideas Focus on market-driven solutions, business models, entrepreneurial ventures, and commercial innovations. Present ideas that address market gaps, improve business processes, or create new revenue streams.
Technical Ideas Showcase cutting-edge technology solutions, software innovations, hardware prototypes, and technical breakthroughs. Present ideas involving AI, blockchain, IoT, cybersecurity, or any emerging technology.

Competition Timeline

01
Registration & Idea Submission Registration opens on 20th September 2025 and closes on 30th September 2025. Submit your idea title and detailed description including problem statement, proposed solution, target audience, potential impact, and all necessary details through the registration form.
↳ Shortlisted participants will be announced on 30th September 2025
02
PPT Submission Phase From 1st October to 6th October 2025, shortlisted participants must submit their comprehensive PPT with detailed solution architecture, implementation strategy, market analysis, and expected outcomes. Selection for finals will be based on your idea quality and presentation.
↳ Finalist participants will be announced on 7th October 2025
03
Final Presentation Round On 11th October 2025, finalists will present their ideas live to expert judges. • Offline Presentation – Mandsaur University students must be physically present on campus. • Online Presentation – Students from other universities across India can present virtually. This hybrid format ensures nationwide participation while maintaining quality evaluation.
↳ Compete for the grand prize and showcase your innovation to industry experts

How to Register

Step 1: Individual Registration This is an individual competition. Register yourself with your university details and course information. Open to students from all universities across India and all academic disciplines.
Step 2: Idea Title & Description Submit your innovative idea title and comprehensive description through the registration form. Include problem statement, solution approach, target audience, and potential impact with all necessary details.
Step 3: Confirmation & Guidelines Receive confirmation email with competition guidelines, evaluation criteria, submission formats, and detailed timeline for all three phases.
